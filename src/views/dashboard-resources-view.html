LEVEL’s Performance Enablement Program is in place as a conduit for Team Member and Manager relationships, where Team Members work to grow their career through meeting and surpassing goals and expectations set each year.

These resources are here as a quick reference to LEVEL’s standards and expectations for all of our team members. For further details, please refer to the Performance Management section of LEVEL’s intranet, or this brief on the Performance Enablement Program

Goal Setting Form

Each Team Member will be asked to complete a goal-setting form in September. However, setting, tracking, and updating goals should be a year-round process.

Peer Feedback Form

Providing supporting comments at the bottom of the form is crucial to creating a deeper level picture of the team member’s performance.

Halfway Check-In Form

The check-in form is sent to all Managers for their completion for each of their Team Members.
