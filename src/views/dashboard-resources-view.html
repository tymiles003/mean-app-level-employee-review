Performance Enablement Forms

LEVEL’s Performance Enablement Program is in place as a conduit for team member and manager relationships, where team members work to grow their career through meeting and surpassing goals and expectations set each year.

These resources are here as a quick reference to LEVEL’s standards and expectations for all team members. For further details, please refer to the Performance Management section of LEVEL’s intranet, or this brief on the Performance Enablement Program

Goal Setting Form

Each team member will be asked to complete a goal-setting form in September. However, setting, tracking, and updating goals should be a year-round process.

Peer Feedback Form

Performance feedback will be collected three times a year from selected peers in April, August, and December. Providing supporting comments at the bottom of the form is crucial to creating a deeper level picture of the team member’s performance.

Halfway Check-In Form

The check-in form is sent to all managers in January for each of their team members. Managers provide constructive observations and a nine- box rating based on their own interactions and the feedback received up to that point in the review year.

Self-Assessment Form

Each team member provides a detailed update of how they tracked against their goals for the year, their accomplishments, growth, and areas for opportunity in each of the performance standard areas, and a self nine-box rating with supporting comments.

Annual Review Form

Based on feedback received for the year and their observations, managers will provide comments for each of the performance standard areas and a nine-box rating with supporting comments.
