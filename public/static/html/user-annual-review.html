Performance Standards

The following standards are used in all phases of the Performance Enablement Program to guide and align Level Team Members in achieving their goals and growing their careers.

    RESULTS

  • Acts on opportunities
  • Committed to excellence
  • Attentive to detail
  • Meets commitments

    EXPERTISE

  • Understands role
  • Seeks improvement
  • Has necessary skills
  • Manages tasks autonomously

    COLLABORATION

  • Shares information and ideas
  • Embodies the Level Values

The 9-box grid is an individual assessment tool that evaluates both a Team Member's current contribution to the Agency and his or her potential level of contribution to the Agency.

When selecting a rating, consider recent performance and the individuals growth and development in the three standards: Results, Expertise, and Collaboration.

Please list details outlining your annual goal status', as well as your accomplishments and growth opportunities for this past performance year within the 3 standards below: results, expertise, and collaboration.
